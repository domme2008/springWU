About Metal Audio

Metal Audio is built for people who care about sound. Whether you're an audio enthusiast, a builder, or someone searching for the perfect setup, our goal is simple: make high-quality audio equipment and reliable information easy to find.

We specialize in speakers and amplifiers designed to deliver powerful, detailed sound. From compact listening setups to larger systems, Metal Audio focuses on products that combine performance, durability, and great design.

Beyond selling equipment, Metal Audio is also a resource for the audio community. Our speaker archive is a growing database where you can explore speaker models, compare specifications, and learn more about the technology behind great sound. It’s built for anyone who wants deeper insight into speakers — whether you're researching before a purchase, restoring older equipment, or simply curious about audio engineering.

At Metal Audio, we believe good sound should be accessible, well-documented, and built to last.
